Privacy Policy

Welcome to World Atlas & Country Info. We value your privacy and are committed to being fully transparent about how we handle information in our application. This policy outlines our data practices in simple, clear language.

✅ No Data Collection
✅ No Ads
✅ Safe for All Ages
✅ Local Processing
🗺️

1. Overview

World Atlas & Country Info is an educational tool designed for exploring world geography and statistics. Our primary goal is to provide rich information while maintaining a minimal data footprint on your device.

🔒

2. Data Collection

Personal Data: We do NOT collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, phone number, or contacts.

Location Data: The app uses map engines (MapLibre / Google Maps) to display geographical data. It does not track or store your real-time GPS location unless you explicitly grant permission to the map provider for the "Find my location" feature.

Device Information: We do not collect device-specific identifiers for marketing or analytics purposes.

🔗

3. Third-Party Services

Our app integrates the following third-party services to enhance the mapping experience:

  • 🗾 Google Maps API: If you choose the Google Maps provider in settings, Google may collect usage data as per their Privacy Policy.
  • 🌐 MapLibre GL: An open-source rendering engine that does not track personal user data by default.
💾

4. Data Usage

All data processing within the app — such as comparing countries or searching for statistics — happens locally on your device or via static JSON assets bundled with the app. We do not operate a backend server that stores your search history or user preferences.

👶

5. Children's Privacy

This app is safe for all ages and provides educational content about world geography. We do not knowingly collect any data from children under the age of 13, in compliance with COPPA guidelines.

📋

6. Permissions

The app may request the following system permissions:

  • 🌐 Internet: To load map tiles and external images like Coat of Arms / emblems.
  • 📡 Network State: To verify if the device is online before attempting map rendering.
📝

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date at the top. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
