Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 26, 2026

Welcome to World Atlas & Country Info. We value your privacy and are committed to being transparent about how we handle information in our application.

1. Overview

World Atlas & Country Info (com.nivedh.WorldAtlasCountryInfo.app) is an educational tool designed for exploring world geography and statistics. Our primary goal is to provide information while maintaining a minimal data footprint on your device.

2. Data Collection

Personal Data: We do NOT collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, phone number, or contacts.

Location Data: The app uses map engines (MapLibre/Google Maps) to display geographical data. While the app shows world maps, it does not track or store your real-time GPS location unless you explicitly grant permission to the map provider for "Find my location" features.

Device Information: We do not collect device-specific identifiers for marketing purposes.

3. Third-Party Services

Our app integrates the following third-party services to enhance the mapping experience:

  • Google Maps API: If you choose to use the Google Maps provider in settings, Google may collect usage data as per their Privacy Policy.
  • MapLibre GL: This is an open-source engine and does not track personal user data by default.

4. Data Usage

Any data processed within the app (such as comparing two countries or searching for statistics) happens locally on your device or via static JSON assets. We do not have a backend server that stores your search history or preferences.

5. Children's Privacy

This app is safe for all ages and provides educational content about world geography. We do not knowingly collect any data from children under the age of 13.

6. Permissions

The app may request the following permissions:

  • Internet: To load map tiles and external images like Coat of Arms/emblems.
  • Network State: To verify if the device is online for map rendering.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
