1. Overview

World Atlas & Country Info is an educational tool designed for exploring world geography and statistics. Our primary goal is to provide rich information while maintaining a minimal data footprint on your device. The app is free to use and supported by advertisements.

🔒

2. Data Collection

Personal Data: We do NOT automatically collect any personally identifiable information (PII). The only personal data we may receive is what you voluntarily submit through our in-app feedback form.

Location Data: The app uses map engines (MapLibre / Google Maps) to display geographical data. It does not track or store your real-time GPS location unless you explicitly grant permission to the map provider for the "Find my location" feature.

Device Information: We do not collect device-specific identifiers directly. However, our advertising partner (Google AdMob) may collect certain device information to serve relevant ads — see Section 3.

📢

3. Advertising

This app is free and ad-supported. We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads depending on your region and consent settings.

AdMob may collect:

  • 📱 Advertising ID: A resettable device identifier used for ad targeting and frequency capping.
  • 🌐 IP Address & Usage Data: Used to determine approximate location and serve relevant ads.
  • 🍪 Cookies & Tracking Technologies: Used by Google to measure ad performance.

For users in the EU/EEA, the app will request your consent before showing personalized ads, in compliance with GDPR. You can review Google's data practices at their Privacy Policy and manage your ad preferences via Google Ad Settings.

💬

4. User Feedback

The app includes an optional Feedback Form to help us improve your experience. The following fields are available:

Field Required? How It's Used
Name Optional To personalize our reply to you (if you provide your email).
Email Optional To respond to your feedback or follow up. Never used for marketing.
Message Required Your feedback, suggestions, or bug report to help us improve the app.
⚠️ Feedback is sent directly to our support email. We do not share your feedback data with any third party. If you include your email, we will only use it to respond to your message.
🔗

5. Third-Party Services

Our app integrates the following third-party services:

  • 📢 Google AdMob: Serves in-app advertisements. May collect device and usage data. See Google Privacy Policy.
  • 🗾 Google Maps API: If you choose the Google Maps provider in settings, Google may collect usage data as per their Privacy Policy.
  • 🌐 MapLibre GL: An open-source rendering engine that does not track personal user data by default.
💾

6. Data Usage

All app functionality — such as comparing countries or searching for statistics — is processed locally on your device via static JSON assets. We do not operate a backend server that stores your usage history or preferences.

Feedback data (if submitted) is received by us solely to respond and improve the app. It is never sold, rented, or shared with advertisers or third parties.

👶

7. Children's Privacy

This app provides educational content about world geography and is intended for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 13.

Ads shown in the app are served by Google AdMob. For users identified as children, Google will serve non-personalized, child-directed ads in compliance with COPPA and applicable laws.

📋

8. Permissions

The app may request the following system permissions:

  • 🌐 Internet: To load map tiles, external images (Coat of Arms / emblems), and display advertisements.
  • 📡 Network State: To verify if the device is online before attempting map rendering.
  • 🆔 AD_ID (Android): Used by Google AdMob to serve and measure advertisements. You can reset or opt out of this via your device's advertising settings.
📝

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date at the top. We encourage you to review this page periodically for any updates.
